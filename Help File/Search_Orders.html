How to process searching for orders

After you successfully log in to the system as a manager or an administrator, you can access order information by choosing the required options in the form.

To open the form, click on the Search Menu and choose the "By Order " . The "Order Search " form will be appeared.

In this form, you can search by orderID, DesignNo, OrderDate, Customer, Type of Sale with the options for either Orders only or Job confirmed or both. After choosing the options, click "Search" button or Alt+S.

To view as a report format, click "Print Preview" button or Alt+P.

To close the form, click "Close" button or Alt+C.
