How to process design data

After you have successfully logged in to the system, you can enter the required data.

First of all, to enter the design data, click the Process menu from the menu bar. The Design form will be appeared.

When it is come out, the design No is automatically filled and only design name , employee name and any remark need to be filled after choosing the required design image by clicking "Get Image" button or "Alt+I" and filled the desirable width and length of the whole toys.

Then, only item information for that toys need to be entered. Firstly, the item code need to be entered. As soon as the item code is entered, its name can be seen and the required item width, length and quatity and any further description.

After that, to save the design, click save button or Alt+S. And the following message will appeared.

In the design form, both editing the data and removing data are not allowed to keep the data right and accurate. Therefore, after entering all information of design, press "Close" or "Alt +C" to exit the design form.
