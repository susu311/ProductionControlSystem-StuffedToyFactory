Help for Production Control System for Stuffed Toys

There are a lot of functions done in the factory such as ordering, order receiving, purchasing materials, cutting, sewing, stitching, stuffing, packaging and

delivering. In this project, I expect to focus on the ordering, order receiving, controlling payment, purchasing materials and making records of cutting, sewing,

stuffing and packaging quantities. In addition, it is necessary to be more accurate records of employees and more specific orders information by particular

customerswith a month or a year in a very short time. I hope it will help to control the system in the main functions and data and they will realize and recognize

several advantages of changing manual system to computerized, systematic system. Firstly, all users must be login with thier own user name and password.

When they enter into the program, they can access the forms according to their login status. But, all users can enter new data in the employees form,

customers form and item form , order form, job confirmation, job delivery, payment and purchasing. The users who only enter as a manager or administrator

can access the searching forms with suitable reports.
